About Our Boosters Club

Our Mission

We are dedicated to supporting our dance program by raising funds, volunteering time, and building community connections that help our dancers reach their full potential.

What We Do

From organizing fundraisers to providing volunteer support at competitions, we work tirelessly to ensure our dance program has the resources needed to excel.

Get Involved
